Intelligent Investor Framework

üü¢ Bullish Tailwinds

India builds DCs at $7/W vs $10/W US ‚Äî massive FDI magnet attracting global capital.
Government's IndiaAI Mission (‚Çπ10,372 Cr) creating guaranteed demand for DC operators.
Data localization laws mandating Indian DCs ‚Äî regulatory moat for domestic operators.
AI workloads expected to grow from <1% to 15-20% of total DC load by 2030 ‚Äî huge revenue jump.
India's power surplus (452GW) removes the classic infrastructure bottleneck.
Global hyperscalers (Google, Microsoft, AWS) committed billions ‚Äî demand is real and growing.

üî¥ Key Risk Factors

Adani Group governance overhang post-Hindenburg ‚Äî elevated political & regulatory risk.
Most Indian DC pure-plays are unprofitable (Sify EPS ~0) ‚Äî valuation depends on future growth.
Massive CapEx cycles may stretch balance sheets ‚Äî rising interest rates can be a headwind.
Bharti Airtel Q3 FY26 net profit fell 55% ‚Äî near-term earnings pressure despite revenue growth.
Global VC/PE capital may rotate away from emerging markets ‚Äî FX and liquidity risk.
Water scarcity concerns for cooling in certain regions ‚Äî ESG risk escalating for large DCs.

‚ë† TCS (NSE: TCS) ‚Äî Steady Compounder
